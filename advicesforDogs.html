Advices for your Dog

    Caring for your dog at home requires regular attention and care. Here are some specific tips for taking good care of your dog at home: Show Cat advices



  • Feeding routine : Feed your dog at regular times and with portions adapted to his size, age, and activity level. Make sure he always has clean, fresh water available.

  • Home hygiene : Brushing : Brush him regularly to prevent matting and reduce shedding. The type of brush depends on your dog's coat./li>
  • Bathing : Bathe him only when necessary (usually every 1-3 months) with a shampoo specially designed for dogs.

  • Ear and eye care : Clean his ears with a suitable product and check for redness or bad odor. Gently wipe his eyes to avoid irritation.

  • Nail Trimming : Trim your dog's nails if they don't wear down naturally. Be careful not to cut them too short to avoid damaging blood vessels.

  • Physical Activity : - Indoor Play : In addition to walks, play indoor games with your dog, such as throwing a ball or an interactive toy.
